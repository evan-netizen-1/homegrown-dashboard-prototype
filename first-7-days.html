3. Annual Conversion Strategy

73% of vendors already choose the annual plan without any active promotion. Here's how to push that higher:

TouchpointStrategy
Day 6 email (V17b) Lead with annual: "Keep your store — $10/mo" as primary CTA. Monthly ($12.50) as secondary, smaller text.
Day 7 email (V17c) Same dual-CTA structure. Annual primary, monthly secondary.
Post-first-order celebration Include annual nudge: "Lock in $10/mo for a year." Ride the dopamine of the first sale.
Day 30 — Monthly vendors (V46) Annual Upgrade Nudge: "You've paid $12.50 this month. Switch to annual and save $30/year."
Pricing discipline Never discount. $10/mo is already the lowest in market. 90% of subscriptions sell at full price (Adapty data).

4. What Needs to Happen (Build List)

Three categories ranked by who can execute and priority level.

Category A: Support Person Can Do Now (Intercom)
  1. Remove the tour modal video — 100% of vendors dismiss it immediately
  2. Replace "Business Information" checklist with "Get your first order" (3 steps: Location, Product, Share)
  3. Set checklist notification style to Snippet — less intrusive, higher engagement
Category B: Email Team Can Do Now (SendGrid + Triggers)
  1. Refresh V11 (Share Your Profile) with copy-paste templates — HIGHEST PRIORITY
  2. Register V5, V17b, V17c templates in SendGrid
  3. Work with dev team to build trigger logic for time-delay + completion-state checks
  4. Refresh V1 to change step order (location first, not product first)
Category C: Dev Team (Product Changes)
  1. Auto-placeholder images by category — when vendor selects a category (Bakery, Produce, Honey, etc.), product automatically gets a category-matched placeholder image. Product is publishable immediately. Real photo replaces placeholder when uploaded. Microcopy under image area: "Don't worry, you can add a photo or edit this later." Dev effort: one conditional — onCategorySelect → set product.image = categoryPlaceholders[category]
  2. Reduce product form to 3 visible fields (Name, Category, Price)
  3. Add trial countdown to dashboard
  4. Fix premature validation on product form
  5. Hide "Add Location" banner during product creation
  6. Add 3 Intercom trackEvent calls (location_added, product_added, store_link_shared)
  7. Replace tour modal with progress checklist in dashboard
  8. Add first-product celebration moment

6. Key Gaps Flagged

  • 14 of 16 planned emails exist as HTML but are NOT registered or triggered — no backend automation is in place for the nudge sequence.
  • "72 hours to first order" claim in V1 welcome email is unverified — 0 orders confirmed in session recordings.
  • Email template file numbering doesn't match strategy doc numbering — causes confusion when cross-referencing templates and plans.
  • Share rate is entirely untracked — the most critical activation action (sharing the store link) has no measurement whatsoever.
  • ICP segmentation has not been applied to any live onboarding touchpoint — all vendors get the same generic flow.
  • No backend trigger logic exists for the nudge email sequence — even if templates are registered in SendGrid, they can't fire without dev work on time-delay + completion-state checks.
